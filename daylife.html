The Road of Hockey

I first got into ice hockey when I was seven.

I joined my first club "Century Star" when I was eight.

In the fourth grade, I started playing for my elementary school's team.

I went to Beijing No. 101 Middle School and started playing for the school team.

I was selected by the Team of Beijing and had won u16 and u18 national title in China.

I represented Hebei Province in the National Championship, which was the highest level of ice hockey competition in China at that time.

At the same time, I entered Beijing No. 101 High School and helped the school team won the Beijing highschool championship.

I transferred to St. Mary's Prep and continued to play for the school team.

I come to the University of Wisconsin-Madison and played for the Badgers club hockey.

At the same time, I was also a referee, officiating U6 to U12 ice hockey games in my spare time.
